Wistia

Margot is Customer Success Manager at the video hosting and analytics platform, Wistia! Margot works strategically with Wistia customers to help them reach their goals. She previously worked in content marketing and paid search consulting at WordStream. Margot specializes in video marketing, social media, paid online advertising, and content marketing. She is passionate about sharing her marketing knowledge through writing and is a regular contributor to WordStream and the Wistia blogs, and has been featured in several other online publications. Margot has a variety of past speaking engagements under her belt including WistiaFest, SMX East, SMX Advanced, SMX West and HeroConf Portland. She was recently included in a list of 41 Customer Success Managers You Should Follow. During Margot's free time she loves to run, cook, and travel.
