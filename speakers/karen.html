Karen Arnold is a Happiness Engineer at Automattic, on the Happiness Engineer Hiring Team. She blogs at karenalma.com and has spoken at WordCamps in the US and Latin America about the WordPress community. She lives in New Mexico where she helps to organize the local WordPress meetup group.
