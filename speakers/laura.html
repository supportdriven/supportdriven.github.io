Laura Marciano

Livestream

Laura Marciano is the Head of Customer Operations at Livestream, where she has worked since 2010. She is responsible for designing and implementing a delightful service and support experience for Livestream customers.

In addition to scaling the Livestream Customer Care team, Laura has helped grow a culture of customer empathy and is constantly implementing new processes and ideas to improve customer success.

A Buffalo, New York native, Laura relocated to New York City in 2006. She holds a degree from the State University of New York at Fredonia in Communications.

When she isn’t glued to her computer, Laura can be found at the park with her two dogs, sampling new restaurant menus in the 5 boroughs, and trying very hard not to binge watch reality TV.
