For the last eight years, I've served as the Vice President of Customer Care at Squarespace. I was the first member of the Customer Care department, so I had to build the department from the ground up, creating and implementing all of our practices and policies and hiring, then training the whole department. I oversaw the department's expansion to 200 associates in three international locations (New York, Dublin, and Portland).

Customer service is my passion and I’ve dedicated my professional career to creating processes, scaling teams, and most importantly connecting people and putting them on a path that makes their work as rewarding as mine.
