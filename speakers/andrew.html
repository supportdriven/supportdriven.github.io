Andrew Spittle is a Happiness Engineer at Automattic, makers of WordPress.com, WooCommerce, and Jetpack. Since joining in 2010 he's helped build the team to more than 140 people spanning dozens of countries; and they all work from home. He's worked on the front lines of WordPress.com support, led support hiring, and now helps guide Automattic's support teams. Outside of work he's an avid reader, runner, traveler, and likes to dabble in photography.
