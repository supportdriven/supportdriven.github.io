Nicole Winstone

Hootsuite

Nicole is the Americas Customer Support Manager at Hootsuite where she leads a multilingual team that supports over 12 million customers worldwide through numerous channels, including social. After graduating with her BA from the University of Victoria, which included studying abroad at the University of Granada in Spain, Nicole spent time further honing her customer experience and language skills before starting at Hootsuite as a Customer Advocate. Her passion for customers, and natural leadership skills propelled her into a Shift Lead and then management position where she's been able to maintain world-class customer satisfaction delivered by her team through Hootsuite's exponential growth.
