TJ Stein

Media Temple

TJ Stein is in charge of all things customer support at Media Temple, a customer-first company providing premium web and cloud hosting solutions to web designers, developers, creative agencies and enterprises. Since joining Media Temple in 2008, Stein has grown into the head of the customer support department, leading the 110 California-based customer support agents who are providing 24/7 award-winning support to over 125,000 clients in 100 countries.

Stein was instrumental in the 2013 launch of Media Temple’s white glove technical customer care CloudTech, through which certified engineers provide a more in-depth level of services and client support. He is also behind Click-to-Call, a new feature launched in 2014 that enables customers to effortlessly initiate calls to customer service agents within their account admin panel without having to wait on hold.

Prior to Media Temple, Stein served as the online content manager for the St. Augustine Record and the online editor of Drift Magazine.

In his free time, he enjoys long-distance running, cycling and craft beers.
